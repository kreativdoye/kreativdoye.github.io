Welcome to our Website

Welcome to the official website of the Nigeria-USA Export and Investment Group, the hub of value-driven investors in West Africa strengthening trade ties with the USA. The Nigeria-USA Export and Investment Group is an indigenous company with a vision of cementing Nigeria's position as the strongest trading partner of the United States of America in Africa. We desire to see that positive trade relationship bring transformative development to Nigeria through increased investments and sustainability to the United States through a stable supply chain - a symbiosis that makes for a prosperous people of Nigeria and a happy people of America.


Our areas of interest

As a value-driven outfit we are sector agnostic and our focus is on Nigeria. However, we look particularly to sectors with high growth like:
  • Energy (especially from Renewable sources)
  • Import and Export
  • Retail and E-commerce
  • Agriculture
We are always innovating, feel free to contact us.
